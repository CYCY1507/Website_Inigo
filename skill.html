RTU CONNECT

An improved RTU Diaries is the RTU Connect project. designed to provide a centralized platform that would allow the RTU community to communicate with one another more readily. It attempts to deal with the difficulties Considering hectic college life and the possibility of student isolation caused by offering a communication and collaboration platform on the internet. The platform's main goals are to support instructors, groups, and groups that interact and communicate with members of the RTU community among themselves.
My role in this project was to design the pages, choose the ideal color scheme, and determine which portions required revisions to the design.
